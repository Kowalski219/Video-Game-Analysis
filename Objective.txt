Objective:
 The project aims to derive valuable insights about video games, their reception, and trends over time.

Dataset Overview:
The dataset contains information about video games including their names, platforms, release dates, summaries, Metascores, and userscores.

What is the Distribution of Metascores and Userscores?

Is There a Correlation Between Metascores and Userscores?

How Have Metascores Changed Over Time?

Which Genres Receive the Highest Metascores?

What Are the Top-Rated Games Overall?

What is the Distribution of Game Summaries' Length?
